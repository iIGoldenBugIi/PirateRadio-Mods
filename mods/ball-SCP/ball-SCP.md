# SCP-Ball

A simple re-texture of the basic ball to show the logo of the SCP-Foundation.

![SCP-Ball](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KnockoutCity-Pirate-Radio/PirateRadio-Mods/refs/heads/main/mods/ball-SCP/SCP-Ball.png)

## What it changes

- `basic_ball_albedo` - Replaces the ball's color/albedo texture
- `basic_ball_normals` - Replaces the ball's normal map for proper lighting
