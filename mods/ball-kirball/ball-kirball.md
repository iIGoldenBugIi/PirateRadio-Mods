# Kirball

Poyo! A reskin of the basic dodgeball to replace it with Kirby!!

![Kirball Preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KnockoutCity-Pirate-Radio/PirateRadio-Mods/refs/heads/main/mods/ball-kirball/ball-kirball.png)

## What it changes

- `basic_ball_albedo` - Replaces the ball's color/albedo texture
- `basic_ball_normals` - Replaces the ball's normal map for proper lighting
