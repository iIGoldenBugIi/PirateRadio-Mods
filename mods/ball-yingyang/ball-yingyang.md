# YingYang Ball

A simple re-texture of the basic ball into a YingYang (Black/White) one.

![YingYang Ball](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KnockoutCity-Pirate-Radio/PirateRadio-Mods/refs/heads/main/mods/ball-yingyang/ball-yingyang.png)

## What it changes

- `basic_ball_albedo` - Replaces the ball's color/albedo texture
- `basic_ball_normals` - Replaces the ball's normal map for proper lighting
